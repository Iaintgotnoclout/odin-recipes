Cajun Cajun Chicken Pasta

Creamy Cajun Chicken Pasta is the perfect cajun chicken alfredo dish made with tender chicken tossed in cajun seasoning in a rich, creamy alfredo sauce. The perfect spicy chicken pasta!

Necessary Ingridients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Pasta
  • Butter and Oil
  • Heavy Cream
  • Chicken Broth
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Red, Orange, and Green Peppers

How To Cook Creamy Cajun Chicken Pasta:

  1. Start by sauteeing onion in butter and oliver oil for 3-4 minutes.
  2. Add the bite-size chicken breast and saute until cooker through about 7-8 minutes
  3. While the chicken is cooking, cook pasta to al dente in salted boiling water.
  4. Once pasta is cooked and drained, toss in with the sauce and fully coat.
  5. Dont forget to garnish with fresh Italian parsley and parmesan cheese.
